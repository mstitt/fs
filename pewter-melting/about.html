Pewter Casting

<-- back to index

I stay with the lead-free pewter ingots. The pewter I have is the slivery looking stuff at the right side of the cookie sheet

(!! WEIGHT?).

Some is in little biscuit shapes (from using the biscuit tin to capture extra melt from the hot pot) and some is in strips, which I make from using the hi-temp silicone mold (the red thing in the lower right corner).

Some of casting sand is in the cookie sheet, and the rest is in the plastic bag.

I sometimes work out a shape within the cookie sheet and just pour into that, or sometimes I pack the sand into the rectangular wooden frames (at the back of the photo).
