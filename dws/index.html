Hi-Tech Diamond 6" Trim Saw

Get your lapidary on! Bought this new a few years back. Works great!

Includes:

  • Three thick blades -- one currently mounted within the saw, and two more (black painted) sitting in front.
  • One thin blade (the 'Red' blade).
  • One medium thickness blade.
  • A home-brew cutting fence I made. Makes it easier to cut strips of a specific width.

Thicker blades cut a straight line more easily but blow away more stone. Thinner blades cut faster and blow away less material, but are a bit harder to keep in a really straight line.
