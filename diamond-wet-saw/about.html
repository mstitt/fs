Diamond Wet Saw

<-- back to index

Bought this new a few years back. Works great.

There is a thick blade in it, and two more spare (black painted). There is also one thin (the 'red' blade) and one medium thickness blade. Thicker blades cut a straight line more easily but blow away more stone. Thinner blades cut faster and blow away less material, but are a bit harder to keep in a really straight line.

Am also including a home-brew cutting fence i made (upper right corner of the photo). Makes it easier to cut strips of a consistent width.
